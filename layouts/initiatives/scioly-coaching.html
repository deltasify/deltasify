Make a Real Impact

The Science Olympiad Coaching Program (SOCP) connects dedicated volunteers with middle schoolers to prepare them for Science Olympiad, a track-meet style academic competition. As a coach, you will dedicate approximately one hour per week to develop lecture materials, research tasks, and practice tests, alongside a one-hour weekly coaching session.

Having prior Science Olympiad experience is preferred, but not a strict requirement. You have the flexibility to coach an event independently or team up with a partner. By aiming for at least two coaches per event, we strive to distribute the workload and accommodate scheduling needs effectively.

Why Coach?

Curious about what teaching is really like? Or passionate about inspiring young minds? This is your chance to make a real impact. As a coach, you’ll design creative lessons on an open-ended topic and guide a small group of middle school students. Few things are more rewarding than watching kids light up while learning!

Beyond the classroom, this experience helps you develop key skills for any teaching-related path — including organization, communication, and leadership. Whether you're considering a future in education or simply love helping others grow, this is a meaningful opportunity to start making a difference.

Schedule & Location

Choose a weekly time slot: Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri (2:30-3:30 PM) or Wed (12:40-1:40 PM).

Location: Riverside STEM Academy (4466 Mt Vernon Ave, Riverside, CA). You have the option to coach online, in-person, or hybrid.

Need flexibility? We understand that summer, winter, or spring obligations may affect your coaching schedule. Let us know if you need to start later or end earlier!
